# Channels: Where the Idea Travels, Stage by Stage

**Executive summary.** Different channels do different jobs: short-form video creates awareness, long-form audio and books create conviction, and broadsheet respectability licenses politicians to engage — so channel strategy is a routing problem, not a megaphone problem. This file maps channels to funnel stages and defines the role of this repository's own destination website as the canonical reference layer.

## The funnel model

| Stage | Audience state | Job of communication | Primary channels |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Awareness** | Has never heard of it | Plant the hook: "£50k at 25" | TikTok/short-form, YouTube, viral press moments |
| **Consideration** | Curious, half-sceptical | Answer the first three objections before they harden | YouTube essays, podcasts, explainer journalism |
| **Conviction** | Persuaded in private | Arm with arguments and identity ("I'm for Universal Inheritance") | Books, long podcasts, the manifesto site, [debate-toolkit.md](debate-toolkit.md) |
| **Action** | Willing to do something | Route into the movement | Owned channels → [13-movement/recruitment.md](../13-movement/recruitment.md) |

## Channel notes

### Books and long-form: the Piketty precedent

*Capital in the Twenty-First Century* (2014) — 700 pages of tax data — became an international bestseller and made "r > g" dinner-party vocabulary ([05-history/piketty.md](../05-history/piketty.md), [03-case-for/piketty-r-vs-g.md](../03-case-for/piketty-r-vs-g.md)). The lesson: a rigorous book can *create* a discourse, and its sales are less important than its citability — thousands of columns cited Piketty for every reader who finished him. A flagship book titled with the provocative flagship name ([naming-and-language.md](naming-and-language.md)) is the natural apex artefact of the [respectability cascade](overton-strategies.md).

### Documentaries: the Inside Job / Social Dilemma model

Both films converted a diffuse, technical grievance (financial engineering; attention engineering) into a named villain-system and a shared reference point. The inheritance equivalent writes itself around the [Founder and the Heir narrative](narratives.md): archive footage of dynasties, the two 25-year-olds rendered as real people, the farm carve-out shown rather than asserted. Documentaries are conviction-stage artefacts that also generate awareness-stage clips.

### Podcasts: converting the educated influentials

Long-form audio is where counterintuitive ideas get three uninterrupted hours — the format that mainstreamed UBI among tech audiences ([overton-strategies.md](overton-strategies.md)). Target the economics/ideas circuit first, then the broad conversational shows. The guest is a [policy entrepreneur](../12-political-adoption/policy-entrepreneurs.md) armed with the [debate-toolkit](debate-toolkit.md); the goal per episode is one host saying, unprompted, "that's actually more reasonable than it sounds."

### YouTube video-essay ecosystem

The 20–40-minute explainer essay is the native habitat of "here's a radical idea, steelmanned." YouTube is a top-five news source for UK 16–24-year-olds — 37% use it for news (Ofcom, 2025) [1] — and generational-injustice and housing content appears to perform strongly in this ecosystem (unverified — no independent channel analytics located), meaning the audience is plausibly pre-assembled. Strategy: brief and resource independent creators rather than building owned channels first — third-party voices carry more trust ([13-movement/messengers.md](../13-movement/messengers.md)) — with the manifesto site as their citation layer.

### TikTok / short-form: the natively viral hook

"Every 25-year-old gets £50,000" is a complete, self-contained short-form hook: concrete, personal, arithmetic-checkable in the comments. Generational-injustice content (boomer housing luck, deposit despair) is already a proven genre. Rules of engagement: endowment only — the tax mechanics do not fit 30 seconds and loss-framing kills sharing; let the comments raise objections and answer them in follow-ups (the objection-answer loop is itself content, and doubles as [inoculation](inoculation.md)).

### Op-eds and broadsheet respectability

Broadsheets do not create mass opinion; they create *permission* — for MPs, editors, and institutions to treat the idea as discussable ([overton-strategies.md](overton-strategies.md)). Sequencing matters: economist-authored pieces in the FT/Times establish seriousness before campaign voices appear in the Guardian; a sympathetic conservative columnist is worth more than five aligned progressive ones ([audience-messaging.md](audience-messaging.md)).

### Local and trade press: the validator circuit

Farmers Weekly, regional dailies, and chamber-of-commerce newsletters are where the farm and family-business attacks will be fought ([10-opposition/media-narratives.md](../10-opposition/media-narratives.md)). The national campaign cannot win there; local validators can — the farmer explaining the carve-out in the county paper pre-empts the auction story ([inoculation.md](inoculation.md)). This channel is defensive but decisive: it is where the opposition's best imagery is manufactured or denied.

### The manifesto website: canonical reference layer

This repository's destination website ([root README](../README.md)) is not a persuasion channel; it is *proof of depth*. Its jobs: (1) give every creator, journalist, and debater a stable URL for every claim, objection, and carve-out — the linkable rebuttal to "they haven't thought this through"; (2) hold the full objection tree ([09-objections/](../09-objections/README.md)) so retail channels can stay simple; (3) convert conviction to action by routing to [13-movement/](../13-movement/README.md). Every artefact in every other channel ends with its URL.

### Paid acquisition: the hand-off

Paid reach (Google/Meta social-issue ads and non-platform paid inventory) is a channel this file deliberately does not own: the spend rules, the flywheel economics (ads that ask viewers to fund more ads), the regulatory gate (platform verification, EU cessation, Electoral Commission thresholds), and the staged pilot all live in [13-movement/paid-acquisition.md](../13-movement/paid-acquisition.md). This file's jurisdiction ends at what the paid creative *says* — the pitches ([pitches.md](pitches.md)), the segment matrix ([audience-messaging.md](audience-messaging.md)), and the inoculation-before-scale rule below, all of which bind paid ads exactly as they bind organic content.

## Sequencing

Respectability channels (books, broadsheets, academia) must be seeded *before* mass channels scale — see the cascade in [overton-strategies.md](overton-strategies.md) — and inoculation content must precede any awareness spike ([inoculation.md](inoculation.md)). Virality without a reference layer and prebunked audiences is a gift to the opposition.
